SATURDAY 31st JULY 1937

Not talking

   I am 2. Mammy is worried because I do not talk. The doctor said don’t worry he will talk when he wants to.

FRIDAY 19th NOVEMBER 1937

Something behind the wardrobe

   There is something behind the wardrobe in the room where I sleep. I am very frightened and do not want to go to bed. Daddy has put a little lamp by my bed. He lights it and turns the wick up. It makes a funny smell. Daddy sings a luller by, but there is something behind the wardrobe.
